    • Mission Statement

    My mission has always been to develop students into artists. It’s my belief that artists inspire their communities with not only the work they produce, but by their very presence. Because of their training, artists have an enriched experience of life and can see connections and beauty where others cannot. The presence of artists profoundly changes the trajectory of communities: from events and nightlife, to the usage and beautification of public spaces, to dialogues about a community’s present and future. Whether it be through songwriting or as an instrumentalist, I challenge my students to engage with their worlds open heartedly and fully express their experiences through music.

    My teaching is centered upon:

    • Learning classic solos
    • Improvisation
    • Music theory
    • Technique
    • Songwriting strategies
    • Fingerstyle playing
    • Music history
